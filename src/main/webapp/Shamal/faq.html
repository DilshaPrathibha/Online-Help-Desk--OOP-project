What is the purpose of the Help Desk?

The Help Desk is a platform where students and faculty can ask questions and get support on various topics related to academic and administrative issues.

How do I submit a support ticket?

You can submit a support ticket by logging into your account and navigating to the 'Submit a Ticket' section, where you will provide details of your issue.
